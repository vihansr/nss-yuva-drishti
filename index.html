Key Findings

High Intent, Low Action

While 95% of youth acknowledge their duty, less than 20% actively participate in social initiatives.

The Fear Factor

Fear of social judgment and backlash is the #1 barrier, surpassing lack of time or money.

Invisible Discrimination

Youth identified 'Mental Health' and 'Economic Class' as the most ignored forms of inequality.
